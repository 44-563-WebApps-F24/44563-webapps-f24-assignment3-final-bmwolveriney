Once a simple mountaineer exploring the icy peaks of the Himalayas, Liam Frost discovered an ancient crystal buried in the ice. When he touched it, a surge of energy transformed him into the Blue Yeti, a powerful guardian imbued with the abilities of the mystical creature. Now, he protects the world's natural wonders from those who would exploit them

Superpowers of Blue Yeti:

  • Ice Manipulation
  • Super Strength
  • Telepathic Connection
