Alaska

Alaska, the largest U.S. state, is known for its stunning natural beauty, including mountains, glaciers, and diverse wildlife. Located in the far north, it has varied climate zones and is economically driven by oil and tourism. With a rich Indigenous heritage and dramatic daylight variations, Alaska offers unique landscapes and outdoor adventures.

Top 3 Places to Visit in Alaska:

  1. Denali National Park
  2. Kenai Fjords National Park
  3. Glacier Bay National Park

Places to Visit Near Alaska:

  • Prince William Sound
    • Stunning fjords and glaciers
    • Excellent wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Talkeetna
  • Hatcher Pass
